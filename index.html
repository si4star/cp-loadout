04

FAQ

How does it fit inside a Really Useful Box?

The Loadout sits inside a Really Useful A4 paper tray, which rests on the rim of a 4L, 9L or 35L Really Useful Box — keeping everything accessible on top while your models are stored below. The A4 paper tray is sold separately on this site if you need one.

Does it fit inside a Really Useful 35L box?

Yes — here's an example of The Loadout sitting inside a 35L Really Useful Box.

Are dice included?

No — dice are not included. The tray holds up to 50 × 16mm dice and 20 × 10mm dice, but you'll need to bring your own.

What is PLA and will it warp?

PLA (polylactic acid) is a common 3D-printing filament. It's rigid, lightweight and odourless. It can warp if left in direct sunlight or a very hot environment — don't leave it on a sunny windowsill or in a hot car. Under normal use it's completely robust.

How long until my order is dispatched?

Everything is printed to order. Please allow up to 7 working days for dispatch — we'll email you with your Evri tracking number as soon as it's on its way. Pre-orders for Warhammer 40,000 products will begin shipping from 1st July, subject to printing capacity.

Do you ship outside the UK?

Not currently — we ship to UK addresses only via Evri.

What's the difference between the AoS and 40K token sets?

The token sets are designed for their respective game systems. The Age of Sigmar set includes wound, hit, rend, damage, save and ward tokens plus CP coins. The Warhammer 40,000 set includes tokens specific to 40K rules. Both fit the same caddy racks in The Loadout.
