How it works

Drop it in

The printed tray is sized to sit flush in a Really Useful Boxes A4 paper tray — no chaos, no wasted space.

Then dock The Loadout, drop the tray back on top of your box & the lid clicks shut. Locked in place for transport. Game packed in seconds.
