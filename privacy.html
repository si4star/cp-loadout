What we collect and why

When you place an order we collect your name, email address, delivery address, and order details. We use this to take payment, fulfil and deliver your order, and contact you about it. The lawful basis is performance of our contract with you.

Who we share it with

How long we keep it

Order records are kept for 6 years to meet accounting and tax obligations, then deleted.
